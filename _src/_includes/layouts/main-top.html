{{ title }}

Penn State Erie, The Behrend College

This year, the Keystone DH conference will be hosted by the DIGIT program at Penn State Erie, The Behrend College on May 20 - 22, 2024. The conference theme is play: the fun of exploring, gaming, and trying new things without being sure you understand them. Our theme of "play" celebrates experimentation and immersion in the experience of digital humanities.
